What FarmSmart gives you

📡

Satellite-Powered Insights

FAO & Open-Meteo satellite data gives you daily soil moisture, vegetation health, temperature, and drought alerts — delivered to your phone.

📷

AI Pest Detection

Take a photo of an affected leaf. Our backend calls HuggingFace's state-of-the-art plant disease model — identifies 17 pests & diseases with exact treatment steps.

📅

Smart Farming Calendar

Personalized calendar tells you when to plant, fertilize, irrigate, spray, and harvest for your specific crops and location in Nigeria.

💰

Live Market Prices

Real prices from AFEX Exchange API across Nigerian markets. Bar chart trends. Know exactly where to sell for the best price today.

🌍

4 Languages

English, Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo. Every screen, every alert, every advisory — switch instantly in Settings.

🔄

OTA Updates

Check for updates from within the app. Download and install the latest APK directly — no Google Play needed.

📴

Offline-First

Uses local Drift database. Queue actions when offline, auto-sync when reconnected. Advisories cached for 14 days.

Feedback → GitHub Issues

Send feedback from the app. It posts to a Cloudflare Worker that creates a GitHub Issue. Every voice matters.
